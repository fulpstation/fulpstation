Creates a list of people who are elligible to become disease carriers for the event

Searches through the player list, adding anyone who is elligible to be a disease carrier for the event. This checks for whether or not the candidate is alive, a crewmember, is able to recieve a disease, and whether or not a disease is already present in them. This proc needs to be run at some point to ensure the event has candidates to infect.
