You are an Abductor!

You and your teammate have been chosen by the mothership to go and capture some humans.

There's two of you: the scientist, who must oversee the camera and surgery, and the agent, who must go down on the station to stun and cuff people.

The scientist must utilize his console and science tool, to scan disguises and scan the agent for retrieval if neccesary.

The scientist must look for targets and send down the agent, after the target is captured the scientist must go down and use the science tool to mark as the teleport target.

After that, he must do the experimentation surgery.

The agent must be sent down by the scientist to stun and cuff a target and drag it into a hidden spot and await the scientist.

People wearing tinfoil hats are immune to the sleep effect abductor batons.

Don't be afraid to ask mentors for help using the mentorhelp button.

For further information visit https://tgstation13.org/wiki/Abductor
