You are a Bloodsucker!

You are a Bloodsucker, an undead blood-seeking monster living aboard Space Station 13.

You regenerate your health slowly, you're weak to fire, and you depend on blood to survive. Don't allow your blood to run too low, or you'll enter a Frenzy!

Fear the daylight! Solar flares will bombard the station periodically, and your coffin can guarantee your safety.

Medical and Genetic Analyzers can sell you out, your Masquerade ability will forge results for you to prevent this.

Rest in a Coffin to claim it, and that area, as your lair.

Once you have a Coffin claimed, you will learn new structures in the Tribal section of your crafting menu.

Examine your new structures to see how they work to your advantage.

Beware: Other Bloodsuckers are not necessarily your friends, but your survival may depend on cooperation. Betray them at your own discretion and peril.

Don't be afraid to ask mentors for help using the mentorhelp button.
