You are a Malfunctioning AI!

Similar to your human counterpart, you have been assigned objectives stored within your notes.

You can hack APCs to take control of them, giving you processing power and full control.

You can use your processing power to purchase abilities, ranging from upgraded cameras, upgraded turrets or even a Doomsday Device.

You can hack cyborgs by using the research director's cyborg control console.

You have access to the Syndicate radio channel, use the T radio prefix to access it.

Don't be afraid to ask mentors for help using the mentorhelp button.
