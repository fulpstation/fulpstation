You are a Heretic!

Eldritch ancient energies surge through you! You have been provided with a Codex Cicatrix and Living Heart.

The details of your objective are stored within your notes, to see them use the Notes verb.

The Codex Cicatrix is your main source of knowledge, use it to choose your path and the different artifacts you can manifest with the right knowledge.

The Living Heart is primarily used to assign sacrifice targets for you to seek, if a target is active, you can use it in hand to know their general location.

The Mansus Grasp is your starter spell, which can knockdown targets and apply minor burn damage. Certain paths will give your grasp more abilities.

You might find influences around the station, use your codex on them to research them and gain knowledge, at the cost of making the influences visible to normal crew.

You can use your codex on a 3x3 area to make a rune, used to create artifacts and get sacrifice targets.

Don't be afraid to ask mentors for help using the mentorhelp button.

For further information visit https://tgstation13.org/wiki/Heretic
