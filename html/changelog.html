11 January 2021

coiax updated:

  • Psychologist immunity to supermatter hallucinations, and the ability to make it "calmer", is now provided by a skillchip, rather than innate.

spessbro updated:

  • stops force defaulting to TRUE when opening large cardboard boxes and supplypods

10 January 2021

ArcaneMusic updated:

  • Adds a new device and security bounty. Use the new In-spect Scanner to scan areas on patrol for all new inspection bounties.
  • You can now craft new prisoner suits/skirts with cloth.
  • Removed the bounty for selling riot shotguns.
  • You can now wash uniforms with any kind of restraint to dye a jumpsuit/skirt into a prison jumpsuit/skirt.
  • Adds a bleeping sound used by the In-Spect Scanner.

EgorDinamit updated:

  • fixed a bug with fulltile pod window spawner

JohnWillard updated:

  • Flypeople eyes ignore direction when flashed

Krysonism updated:

  • Vend-a-trays can now accept any item.

prodirus updated:

  • You can now insert food items into a food processor directly from trays again.

spessbro updated:

  • civilian bounty terminals have had a hotfix
  • fixed science goggles not displaying fermenting barrel contents

09 January 2021

Krysonism updated:

  • Added a new cytology clown mob, the banana glutton!
  • Removed the old clown blob mob.
  • The cytology clown mob cell lines now have properly assigned reagents.
  • The old clown blob sprite has been partially resprited.

LemonInTheDark updated:

  • Chat messages that are more then text, so videos, embedded games, font changes, etc now get wiped when the chat is reloaded. NO MORE INFINITE WINGDINGS!
  • Speeds up gas movement
  • Documents the intent and finer details of the atmos system (Thanks dunc/aran/skog)
  • Fixes excited groups constantly rebuilding, this broke 4 years ago
  • Fixes superconduction just straight up not working. This means heat can leak through windows, glass doors, and a few other things. Keep an eye on your temperature
  • Most heat based behavior no longer relies on being inside a fire
  • APCs take damage in heat to prevent infinite plasma fire diffs, watch out when operating that supermatter
  • Cleans up immutable gas mixtures to make them work properly when the mix has gas in it
  • Planetary turfs no longer create a new copy of themselves each time they process. We instead use a global mmutable mix
  • Cleans up a typed for loop in reactions
  • Canisters will properly take damage from outside heat now
  • Cleans up some roundstart ATs on some ruins
  • Keeps openspace from trying to melt
  • Destroying a wall wakes up the tile beneath it to atmos, no more meteor breaches with no flow
  • Fire alarms will trigger when the area gets too cold for humans

Melbert updated:

  • Grafts of chemical genes in botany now actually work

Qustinnus updated:

  • Fixes slimes falling off monkeys

Timberpoes updated:

  • Certain ID card access changes are now game logged with an accompanying admin notification and entry in the player's logging panel under "Show All"

dragomagol updated:

  • Checking "escape on the emergency shuttle without being in custody" is done by area, not by turf

interestingusernam3 updated:

  • Turning into a monkey now makes you drop all cuffs again.
  • Mimics can no longer eat an unlimited amount of stuff and are instead limited to 10 mobs and 50 things overall. This means player mimics are no longer able to cause significant lag by opening and closing themselves.

necromanceranne updated:

  • Line change to fix monkeys never devolving into humans.

zxaber updated:

  • AIs that lose power can access their APC once more (after the same short hacking delay is complete).
