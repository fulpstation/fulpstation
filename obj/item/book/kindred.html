Archives of the Kindred:
/obj/item/book/kindred

A book that can only be used by Curators. When used on a player, after a short timer, will reveal if the player is a Bloodsucker, including their real name and Clan. This book should not work on Bloodsuckers using the Masquerade ability. If it reveals a Bloodsucker, the Curator will then be able to tell they are a Bloodsucker on examine (Like a Vassal). Reading it normally will allow Curators to read what each Clan does, with some extra flavor text ones.

Regular Bloodsuckers won't have any negative effects from the book, while everyone else will get burns/eye damage.
